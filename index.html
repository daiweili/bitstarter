Explore your own backyard

In CityGuessr, the player is presented with an image of a location of a landmark of their city, and a map for them to guess where in the city the landmark is in. This lets players from the same city compete on how well they know the local landscape and hopefully discover new places to explore in their own backyard! Users will also be able to create their own set of locations and corresponding StreetView images to share with friends.

Background

CityGuessr is inspired by GeoGuessr, a really cool location guessing game created by Anton Wall√©n where the player is presented with a random image from Google StreetView, and attempts to guess where in the world this image is from.
